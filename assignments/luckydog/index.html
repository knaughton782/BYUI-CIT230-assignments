Stay in Our Comfortable Rooms

Accommodations at Temple Inn & Suites feature contemporary and peaceful design. All rooms include a 26-inch plasma satellite TV, a work desk and a private bathroom with hairdryer. Various room sizes available, including family suites.

Conveniently located for visiting the Washington DC Temple

The Temple Inn & Suites provides additional services for those attending the nearby temples. Located conveniently to our Maryland location, the Washington Temple is the 18th constructed temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

Business or Family History Work

The Temple Inn & Suites provides computer, printer, and internet services for those who have business needs, as well as for those who wish to do some Family History Work or print ordinance cards while they are staying. Staff has some training in use of FamilySearch.org.

About Temple Inn & Suites

The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. The owner-operated company caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. The hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company.
