Services Provided by the Temple Inn & Suites

The Temple Inn & Suites provides numerous services for travelers staying at the hotel, as well as services specifically suited for the members of The Church of Latter-day Saints who are in the area to attend the nearby temple.

Hotel Amenities

The Temple Inn & Suites has a full compliment of amenities to make your stay more enjoyable. These include:

  • Complimentary Breakfast
  • Exercise / Workout Room
  • Indoor Pool and Spa
  • Wifi and High Speed Internet
  • Smart TVs in each Room and Common Areas
  • Ample Parking with Security
  • Family History Consultation and Family Ordinance Card Services

Full Time Missionaries

Missionaries serving full-time for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints are often in need of a place to stay that is convenient to their area. The Temple Inn & Suites has suites available with kitchen and laundry services that are uniquely suited to the missionaries of this area.

Convenience for Temple Patrons

Those attending the temple may have need of some of the special services provided by The Temple Inn & Suites, including:

  • Free Access to Changing Rooms and Bathroom Facilities
  • A children's playroom
  • Low cost, short-term babysitting services for children
  • A temple history themed reception and sitting area that features the nearby temple
  • A customizable wedding reception hall with a simple catering menu or kitchen access for food preparation and serving
  • A business center that supports family search and ordinance work

Wedding Receptions

The Temple Inn & Suites is especially suited to wedding receptions or family gatherings. Considering its convenient location to the LDS temple, it is easy to continue the family celebration here.

The reception room is configured for flexible sizing depending on the number of guests. We have a number of decorative items that can easily fit any color scheme with the addition of ribbon or flowers.

Our kitchen has excellent catering services. And for those who prefer to cater their own events, we have a guest kitchen with all the amenities necessary to both prepare and serve snacks, a brunch, or a full meal.
